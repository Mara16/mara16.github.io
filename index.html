Obsmara Ulloa is a Computer Science PhD student at Northwestern University, where she is advised by Maia Jacobs. Obsmara’s research interests include understanding how Human Computer Interaction, Artificial Intelligence and computing can be used to target disparities in healthcare and advance computational literacy.
